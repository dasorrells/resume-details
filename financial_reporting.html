Automated Financial Reporting Process

“Automated daily system-wide sales, transactions, and DPT reporting using Python and Excel, enabling flexible segmentation and robust year-over-year comparisons.”

Overview of the Report

This report provides a comprehensive yet streamlined daily snapshot of system-wide sales (SWS), transactions, and dollars-per-transaction (DPT) by center maturity class (New, Ramping, Mature). It delivers key performance metrics side by side with their year-over-year (YOY) comparisons and organizes the data into intuitive subtotals for easy reference. The final product includes:

  • Page 1: Aggregate view of system-wide sales, transactions, and DPT totals.
  • Page 2: Sales over the last four weeks, split by maturity class and state.
  • Page 3: Transactions over the last four weeks, also split by maturity class and state.

Key Maturity Classes

  • New: 1 to 12 months
  • Ramping: 13 to 59 months
  • Mature: 60+ months

This segmentation highlights growth trajectories and allows quick identification of trends across centers with different operational tenures.

High-Level Process

1. Data Cleaning and Preparation

  • Date Conversion: Convert text-based dates to proper datetime objects (e.g., CENTER_GRAND_OPENING_DATE, DATEKEY), ensuring consistent date formats.
  • Missing Value Handling: Replace or fill null or placeholder values to maintain valid numeric and date columns.

2. Center Matching and Maturity Calculation

  • Merging Center Info: Merge center data from multiple tables to attach crucial attributes (e.g., CENTER_ID, STATE_ABBREV, MANAGING_OWNER), replacing legacy "0000" with a valid placeholder when necessary.
  • Months in Operation (MIO): Compute how long each center has been operating by comparing CENTER_OPENING_DATE to the relevant fiscal date, returning a monthly difference.
  • Maturity Classification: Translate MIO into categories (New, Ramping, Mature) so that all subsequent reporting can easily group performance metrics by center age.

3. Year-over-Year (YOY) Comparison

  • Previous Year Date: For each record in the current fiscal year, calculate the corresponding date a year earlier by subtracting 364 days.
  • Merging Prior Year Data: Bring in last year’s ADJUSTED_DOLLARS_CY, TOTAL_TRANSACTIONS_D_TC, and other key metrics so that each record includes both current-year and prior-year performance for easy side-by-side comparison.

4. Aggregation & Output

  • Grouping by Key Dimensions: Summarize daily performance by maturity class, month, and week to facilitate both daily snapshots and broader weekly/monthly trends.
  • Excel Export: Output aggregated data to an Excel file (Interim_YYYYMMDD.xlsx), making it easy to integrate with a polished reporting shell built in Excel. The final formatted report is then automatically updated simply by pasting the new data.

Benefits and Impact

  • Time Savings: The automation significantly reduces manual steps, ensuring up-to-date metrics and faster turnaround.
  • Consistency & Accuracy: The code enforces standardized data cleanup and merging, minimizing the risk of human error.
  • Actionable Insights: Management can quickly assess performance trends by maturity stage, diagnosing strengths and areas for improvement across the network.

Bottom Line

This automated reporting framework delivers an at-a-glance understanding of daily performance across over 1,000 centers, capturing critical YOY shifts in sales, transactions, and DPT in a consistent, user-friendly format.
